What is PAW?

PAW is a platform that makes it easier for people to find, adopt, and connect with pets in need of a home.

Problem

Vision

A world where every pet has a safe, loving, and permanent home.

Mission

To connect people with pets through a simple, fun, and meaningful adoption process.
